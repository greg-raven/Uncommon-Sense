I think communist subversive activities in the United States today are serious. I think it's a matter that we should continue to give great care and attention to.

We should support the laws which the United States has passed in order to protect us from those who would destroy us from within. We should sustain the Department of Justice in its efforts and the FBI, and we should be continually alert.

I think if the United States is maintaining a strong society here in the United States, I think that we can meet any internal threat.
